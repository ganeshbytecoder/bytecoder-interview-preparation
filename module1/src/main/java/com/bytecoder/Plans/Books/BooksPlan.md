* **"Atomic Habits" by James Clear** – People love this one for self-improvement and habit-building.
* **"How to Win Friends and Influence People" by Dale Carnegie** – It's a classic on communication.
* **"The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People" by Stephen Covey** – Another cornerstone of personal development.
* **"Outliers" by Malcolm Gladwell** – A really interesting one on success and what makes people stand out.
* **"Dare to Lead" by Brené Brown** – Great for leadership and courage.
* **"Deep Work" by Cal Newport** – Super helpful on productivity and focus.
* **"The Power of Now" by Eckhart Tolle** – A great one on mindfulness and living in the present.
* **"Thinking, Fast and Slow" by Daniel Kahneman** – A bit more on the psychology and decision-making side.
* **"Sapiens" by Yuval Noah Harari** – Since you're already doing summaries like that, it's a great one to include.
* **"The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck" by Mark Manson** – Always a crowd-pleaser for a bit of a different tone.
